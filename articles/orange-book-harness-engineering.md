## Overview

An open-source Harness Engineering reference book authored by Huashu (Alchaincyf), presented in "orange book" style. It covers core harness concepts, design patterns, and practical guides.

Published as an open-source project on GitHub, the community can contribute and improve it directly.

---

## Why It Matters

Together with WquGuru's PDF books and "The Horse Book," this forms the trio of core reference readings on Harness Engineering in the Chinese-speaking community. The Orange Book leans toward introductory and panoramic coverage, making it suitable for beginners.
